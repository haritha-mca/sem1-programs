Study important options and uses of following command line tools for networking. ping, traceroute,  netstat, tcpdump, ip, nslookup, route, ifconfig, ifup,, ifquery, curl, wget 

1.	ping: 

                  ping www.goggle.com

       
2.	traceroute: 

                  traceroute  www.goggle.com


3.	netstat: 

                  netstat –u  www.goggle.com


4.	tcpdump:  

                  tcpdump –D


5.	ip: 

                 ip route


6.	nslookup: 

                  nslookup  www.goggle.com


7.	route: 

                 route –n


8.	ifconfig: 

                  ifconfig –v


9.	ifup, ifquery: 

                  ifquery –l

                  ifup –V


10.	curl: 

                 curl –d name=”curl”  www.goggle.com





11.	wget: 
 
              wget  http:// www.goggle.com
